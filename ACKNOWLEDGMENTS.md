# Acknowledgments

AO Rebirth would not exist without the work of the developers, researchers,
testers, and community members who came before us.

We would like to extend our sincere appreciation to the original CellAO project
team and all contributors who dedicated their time and expertise to researching,
documenting, and implementing Anarchy Online server technology over the years.

Their work helped preserve invaluable technical knowledge, created a foundation
for future research, and demonstrated what a dedicated community can accomplish
through collaboration and perseverance.

# Project Heritage

AO Rebirth traces part of its technical heritage to the CellAO project and the
broader community efforts that sought to preserve and understand Anarchy
Online's server architecture and gameplay systems.

While AO Rebirth is an independent project with its own goals, architecture, and
roadmap, we recognize the important role previous community projects played in
advancing research, documentation, and preservation efforts throughout the
Anarchy Online community.
